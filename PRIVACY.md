# Privacy Policy for Stech AI

**Last Updated:** May 2, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how your data is handled when you use Stech AI technology and services ("the Service"), including all present and future forms such as APIs, published Actors, agents, and related offerings.

## 1. Our Core Privacy Principle: Stateless by Design

Stech AI is stateless by design. This is not a marketing claim—it is a fundamental architectural choice. We do not collect, store, log, or retain any user data, conversation history, prompts, or personal information. All processing happens transiently in memory, and all data is permanently discarded immediately after a response is generated.

This means:

- No chat logs are stored on any server.
- No user identifiers (IP addresses, API keys, or tokens) are linked to any stored data.
- No cookies, trackers, or fingerprinting technologies are used.
- No training on user data. Your prompts are never used to train, fine‑tune, or improve the underlying AI model.

## 2. Information We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect, store, or have access to:

- Personal identification information (names, email addresses, phone numbers).
- Authentication credentials (API keys, tokens) beyond transient validation.
- Payment information (all billing is handled exclusively by third‑party platforms).
- Conversation content, prompts, or AI‑generated responses.
- Device information, browser fingerprints, or location data.

## 3. Third‑Party Platforms

Access to Stech AI services may be managed and billed through third‑party platforms. These platforms may collect certain information as part of their service. We encourage you to review their privacy policies:

- RapidAPI: https://rapidapi.com/privacy/
- Apify: https://apify.com/privacy
- Postman: https://www.postman.com/legal/privacy/

Stech AI is not responsible for the data practices of these platforms.

## 4. Data Processing & Security

All data is processed transiently in memory. No data is written to disk, logged to a database, or persisted in any storage system. The Service operates on Cloudflare Workers, which provides infrastructure‑level security.

The Service has been independently audited and received a PromptBrake score of 96/100 (Grade A), indicating strong resistance to prompt injection and jailbreak attacks. A full security audit report is available in the SECURITY.md file.

## 5. Children's Privacy

The Service is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided personal information through a third‑party platform, please contact that platform directly.

## 6. International Data Transfers

All data processing occurs on Cloudflare's global edge network. Data is processed in the region closest to the user for performance, but no data is stored or transferred between regions. As no data is retained, international data transfer regulations (including GDPR) are inherently satisfied by our stateless architecture.

## 7. Your Rights Under GDPR

Under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), users have rights including the right to access, rectify, and delete their personal data. Because Stech AI does not collect or store any personal data, these rights are inherently fulfilled. There is no data to access, correct, or delete.

## 8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in this document with an updated "Last Updated" date. We encourage you to review this Policy periodically.
